How to play
  1. At the center outside a checkerboard, you begin a journey of coin flips to dictate your path to your reward
  2. When you flip a coin, you go left (tails) or right (head)
  3. There are 4 types of tiles: blockades, bridges, death tiles, and reward tiles
    • You cannot move into blockades
    • Bridges let you go over water
    • Death tiles make you loose your data dollar
    • Reward tiles give you data dollars. You can win 1, 2, or 3 data dollars
  4. If you land onto a reward (or death) tile, your game ends!
